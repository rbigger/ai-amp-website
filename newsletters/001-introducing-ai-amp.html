AI Development Shouldn't Be a Black Box

Most AI coding tools operate in the shadows. They write code, but you can't see what they're doing, why they made certain decisions, or prove compliance to auditors.

For teams building software in regulated industries, that's not just inconvenient—it's a dealbreaker.

Meet AI-AMP

AI-AMP is an AI Agent Management Platform that orchestrates teams of specialized AI agents with full accountability, audit trails, and compliance controls.

Instead of one AI trying to do everything, AI-AMP deploys 15 specialized agents—each with defined decision scopes, clear escalation paths, and complete accountability:

ARCHITECT — Creates visions, delegates work, performs independent verification
CODER — Writes tests first (TDD mandatory), ensures 0 build errors before handoff
TESTER — Executes tests, documents results with evidence
CISO — Monitors security alerts, classifies vulnerabilities (SEV0-3)
COMPLIANCE — Collects evidence, maintains traceability matrices
RESCUER — Reconstructs crashed agent context from session files
Plus 9 more specialized roles...

Every agent knows what it can decide and what requires escalation. No agent can approve its own work.

Built for Regulated Industries

✓ Complete Audit Trails — Immutable logs of every agent action
✓ Approval Workflows — Multi-party gates for sensitive operations
✓ Kill Switches — Immediate agent termination when needed
✓ Environment Separation — Strict boundaries between dev, staging, and production

Aligning to SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria. Type I certification targeted for Q2 2026.
